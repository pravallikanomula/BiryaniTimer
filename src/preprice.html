PREP FOR RICE

For Cooking the Rice

  • 1 kg basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • Boil 3–4 liters water on high Flame
  • Once the water is boiled add:
  • 2–3 bay leaves
  • 4–5 cloves
  • 3–4 green cardamoms
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt – to taste
  • Add 1tsp of shahjeera
  • Juice of 1 lemon and add the lemon piece as well
  • 2 green chilies sliced (optional)
  • Add few drops of ghee or oil (optional)
  • After 2 mins add the soaked rice into the boiling water
  • turn the timer on
Pro Tip: Try to be ready with a net to separate the rice and spread out a plastic wrap on the platform or a table to rescue the rice from becoming a cake.
