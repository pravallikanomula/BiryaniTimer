The Perfect Biryani

INGREDIENTS

For Marinating the Chicken

  • 1 kg chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt – to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • A handful of chopped mint leaves
  • A handful of chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)

For Rice

  • 1 kg basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 3–4 liters water (for boiling)
  • 2–3 bay leaves
  • 4–5 cloves
  • 3–4 green cardamoms
  • 1 black cardamom (optional)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt – to taste

For Frying Onions

  • 3–4 large onions (thinly sliced)
  • Oil – for deep frying

For Assembling

  • 4 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • A few saffron strands soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk (or yellow food color)
  • Fried onions (from above)
  • Extra mint and coriander leaves for layering
