PREP FOR ONIONS

Preping the Onions for barista

  • TAKE 3 MEDIUM SIZED ONIONS AND SLICE THEM INTO THIN SLICES.
  • HEAT UP THE OIL AND ADD THE ONIONS.
  • TAKE A PLATE AND ARRANGE SOME PAPER NAPKINS OVER THEM, TO PUT FRIED ONIONS IN IT.
    • Pro Tip: TRY TO TAKE THE ONIONS OUT WHEN THEY ARE SLIGHTLY BROWN. FIRED ONIONS TURN DARK CAUSE OF THE HEAT THEY ABSORB FOR FEW SECONDS AFTER YOU TALK THEM OUT. DONT WAIT UNTILL THEY TURN DARK BROWN.
