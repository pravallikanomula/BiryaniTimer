PREP FOR CHICKEN

For Marinating the Chicken

    Marinating & Cooking the Chicken

    • TAKE THE 1/2 KG CHICKEN AND ADD SALT TO TASTE
    • ADD LEMON JUICE OF 1 NOS.
    • ADD 4 TBSP OF GINGER GARLIC PASTE
    • ADD 3 NOS. OF GREEN CHILLI PASTE
    • ADD KASHMIRI RED CHILLI POWDER – 2 TBSP
    • ADD TURMERIC POWDER – 1/2 TSP
    • ADD CORIANDER POWDER – 2 TSP
    • ADD BIRYANI MASALA – 1.5 TBSP
    • ADD BRISTA OIL – 3–4 TBSP
    • ADD CURD – 200 ML
    • ADD POTATOES – 1 MEDIUM SIZED (OPTIONAL)
    • ADD MINT – SMALL HANDFUL (CHOPPED)
    • ADD FRESH CORIANDER – SMALL HANDFUL (CHOPPED)
    • ADD GHEE – 2 TBSP
      • Pro Tip: Beat the curd until it turns soft and creamy with no chunks and try to marinate the chicken for atleast 30 mins before cooking, marinating over-night is always the best way.
Pro Tip: Try to be ready with a net to separate the rice and spread out a plastic wrap on the platform or a table to rescue the rice from becoming a cake.
